1.US Uniform Crimes

In 2012, 48 law enforcement officers died from injuries incurred in the line of duty. FBI releases the US Uniform Crime Report every year, which offers statistics of those crime involvement, including officers feloniously killed, officers accidentally killed, officers assaulted, and federal officers killed and assaulted from 1996 to 2012.

This topic has not received enough attention from the media, as I had hard time to find related articles. I think these FBI reports are sort of meaningful, thus deserving more coverage.

My project would be an interactive chart showing different aspects of those uniform crime cases across the US. The opening graphic could be a locator map showing the total numbers of the crimes across the US. As readers click through the graphic more details will show up, such as time of the crime, age of the officers, weapons, circumstances, etc.
