Consumer Complain in US

Have you complained your credit card? What are the top reasons people tend to complain their consumption? What are the top financial products people complain more, and what are the deep reasons for those complains? How effective of those complains?

Many people want to know more to solve their financial problems. Since the World Consumer Rights Day is coming soon, which is March 15, the topic will be newsworthy to some degree. The story will be an explainer to explore why people are complaining about their financial products and how the financial institutes response.

Federal Trade Commission issued a reported called Top 10 National Consumer Complains, in which identity thief ranks the first. Meanwhile Consumer Financial Protection Bureau Consumer Complaint Database contains complaints received about financial products and services. It will be interesting to compare the two dataset at the same time.
